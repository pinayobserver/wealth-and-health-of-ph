Wealth and Health of Philippine Provinces

Data Sources: Human Development Network (HDN), National Statistics Office (NSO)

version 0.1, rreyes, uploaded 08/24/2014

This interactive chart shows how long Filipinos live and how much they earn in 78 provinces. Wealthier and healthier provinces live in the upper right region of the chart; poorer and sicker ones live in the lower left region. Population size is shown by the size of the bubbles.

  • Mouse over any bubble to show which province it represents. - Which provinces have the highest income? The highest life expectancy? The lowest?
  • Mouse over the region names (on the right side) to highlight the provinces in the region.- How do the different regions fare as you go from Luzon, Visayas, to Mindanao?

This visualization is directly inspired by Hans Rosling's Wealth & Health of Nations.

The current version shows 2003 values for income and life expectancy and 2010 values for population.
